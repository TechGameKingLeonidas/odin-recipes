Ingredients

  • American cheese
  • Steak
  • Bread
  • Onions and garnishments
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Prepare all items and thaw steak if frozen
  2. Spread cheese sauce over steak
  3. Add onions and garnishments
  4. Grill bread al dente
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes on 450 degrees
  6. Remove from stovetop and let sit 10 minutes to cool
