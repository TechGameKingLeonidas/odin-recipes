Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Feta cheese
  • Lasagna seasoning
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare all items and thaw pasta if frozen
  2. Spread tomato sauce over top of pasta
  3. Add lasagna seasoning
  4. Add feta cheese and salt
  5. Bake for 15 minutes on 450 degrees
  6. Remove from oven and let sit 10 minutes to cool
